About

Tamatea is a 15-year-old artist from Aotearoa New Zealand whose work spans intricate hand-drawn mechanical illustration, pencil art, and digital fiction. His drawings are dense, otherworldly, and unmistakably his own — full of systems and structures that feel like they belong to a world just beyond this one.
